SmartSpray Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 5, 2026

SmartSpray is a turf spray calculation and recordkeeping tool.

SmartSpray does not collect, sell, or share personal information. Spray calculations and records are stored locally on the user's device unless the user manually exports or shares them.

SmartSpray does not use third-party advertising networks and does not track users across apps or websites.

SmartSpray is intended as a calculation and recordkeeping aid only. Users are responsible for following product labels, local regulations, site conditions, and licensed applicator requirements before applying any pesticide, fertilizer, or chemical product.
